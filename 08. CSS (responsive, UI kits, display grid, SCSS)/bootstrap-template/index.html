The Batman

Batman, a vigilante driven by a personal vendetta, emerges as Gotham City's dark protector in the face of overwhelming crime and corruption. As billionaire Bruce Wayne, he struggles with his traumatic past, including the murder of his parents, which fuels his relentless fight against the city's underworld. Disguised as Batman, he battles iconic villains like the Joker, a madman whose goal is to plunge Gotham into chaos, and other dangerous foes who seek to exploit the city's vulnerabilities. Alongside loyal allies like Alfred and Commissioner Gordon, Batman must navigate the complexities of justice, sacrifice, and his own morality. As his identity is put to the test, Batman grapples with the consequences of his vigilantism and the toll it takes on his relationships, while trying to prevent Gotham's complete destruction. Ultimately, he faces a moral dilemma that forces him to question whether the cost of being a hero is worth the price.
